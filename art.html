Urban Enclave: Experimental Design

This Drawing depicts a small Urban Enclave, with a population of about 1000 citizens. This Enclave focused on being more dense than normal, and was an exprimental design rather than a standard template for Enclaves of this size.

Redemption Of Humanity

Following the Defeat of The FSA and the capture of what remained of Boston, the city represnted a milestone in how far the Movement had come. The Nation has continued to recognise its historical importance and thus the city is depicted in many artworks and inspirational posters. The City representing the goals of both redemption and rebirth of humanity! Keep in mind the noticable ammount of greenery, which is due to considerable terraforming and irrigation efforts on the land around the city
